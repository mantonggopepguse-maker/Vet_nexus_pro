

VET-SYNC AI

Embedded AI Agent System for Veterinary Practice Management


---

1. PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

VET-Sync AI is not a chatbot and not a standalone AI tool.
It is a first-class system component embedded inside the veterinary PMS.

Core principles:

AI assists, never replaces veterinarians

AI outputs are drafts, not decisions

Every clinical action remains human-approved

AI lives inside the workflow, not beside it


The goal is simple:

> Remove administrative friction so veterinarians can focus on care.




---

2. OVERALL AGENT STRATEGY

VET-Sync AI is designed as a stack of specialized agents, all accessible through a dedicated AI section in the UI, but activated contextually across the system.

Agents are introduced in phases, increasing in capability and responsibility over time.


---

3. AI AGENT SUITE & ABILITIES


---

AGENT 1: AI SCRIBE / DOCUMENTATION AGENT

(Highest Immediate Value · Foundation Agent)

Purpose

Eliminate manual clinical documentation by converting real exam-room conversations into structured veterinary records.


---

Core Abilities

Listens to live consultations (microphone) or uploaded audio

High-accuracy speech-to-text tuned for veterinary terminology

Generates:

Draft SOAP notes

Visit summaries

Discharge instructions (client-friendly)


Detects and flags missing data (e.g. vitals not mentioned)

Never invents diagnoses, drugs, or dosages


All outputs are clearly marked as DRAFT.


---

Guardrails

No autonomous medical decisions

No final record writing without vet approval

Full transcript always visible for verification



---

Value

Saves 60–120+ minutes per vet per day

Reduces after-hours charting

Improves consistency and auditability of records



---

Market Proof (Comparable Tools)

ScribbleVet

CoVet

HappyDoc



---

AGENT 2: CLIENT COMMUNICATION / FRONT-DESK AGENT

(Revenue & Retention Engine)

Purpose

Act as a 24/7 transactional front desk, handling client communication across channels.


---

Core Abilities

Appointment booking & rescheduling

Automated reminders (vaccines, follow-ups, missed visits)

Refill requests

FAQs (hours, prep instructions, pricing ranges)

Post-visit follow-ups

Escalation of complex cases to human staff



---

Supported Channels

WhatsApp

SMS

Voice calls

Website chat



---

Strict Limits

No diagnosis

No prescriptions

No emergency medical advice
(Only urgency tagging and escalation)



---

Value

Reduces no-shows

Captures missed revenue

Frees reception staff

Enables clinics to scale without immediate hiring



---

Market Proof

---

AGENT 3: WORKFLOW / OPERATIONS AGENT

(Advanced · Platform Differentiator)

Purpose

Perform multi-step operational tasks across PMS modules with human oversight.


---

Core Abilities

Inventory monitoring & shortage prediction

Drafting purchase orders

Schedule conflict detection and optimization

Lab turnaround monitoring

Staff notification and task generation



---

Characteristics

Cross-module awareness

Rule-based + AI-assisted reasoning

Human approval before execution



---

Value

High switching costs

Positions PMS as clinic infrastructure



---

AGENT 4: DIAGNOSTIC / DECISION-SUPPORT AGENT

(Highest Clinical Value · Highest Responsibility)

Purpose

Support veterinarians with clinical insight, not decisions.


---

Core Abilities

Suggests possible differentials based on vet-entered data

Flags red-alert symptoms

Integrates peer-reviewed references

Works alongside labs and imaging



---

Safeguards

No definitive diagnoses

No autonomous prescriptions

Explicit disclaimers

Full vet override

Complete audit trail

Market Proof
